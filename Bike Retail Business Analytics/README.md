# Bike Retail Business Analytics

**Project Overview**  
This project applies analytics to a bicycle retail business to extract meaningful insights from transaction data. We performed analyses including change-over-time trends, cumulative and performance evaluation, segmentation of customers and products.

We created **Customer Reports** and **Product Reports** to help the company identify high-value customers, track sales trends, monitor product performance, and make data-driven business decisions. The project demonstrates practical solutions for revenue growth, improved marketing targeting, and inventory optimization. Key SQL functions were used to implement these analyses efficiently.

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Change Over Time Analysis
**Purpose**: Track business metrics over time.  
**Use Case**: Identify growth trends, seasonal patterns, and key sales periods.

### 2. Cumulative Analysis
**Purpose**: Calculate running totals and moving averages to understand overall progress.  
**Use Case**: Year-to-date revenue tracking, customer base growth, smoothing fluctuations.

### 3. Performance Analysis
**Purpose**: Compare current performance against previous periods.  
**Use Case**: Month-over-month or year-over-year comparisons, flagging declining products or segments.

### 4. Data Segmentation Analysis
**Purpose**: Divide customers and products into meaningful groups.  
**Use Case**: Identify VIP, regular, and new customers; classify high, mid, and low-performing products.

## Customer Report
- Consolidates customer behavior, value, and segmentation  
- Tracks total orders, sales, quantity, product variety, and lifespan  
- Monitors KPIs like recency, average order value, and average monthly spend  
- Helps identify high-value customers, retention opportunities, and win-back campaigns---

## Product Report
- Consolidates product performance metrics and segmentation  
- Tracks total sales, orders, units sold, and unique customers  
- Monitors KPIs like recency, average revenue per order, and monthly revenue  
- Guides inventory decisions, marketing prioritization, and product lifecycle management
